Comic Relief Tech for Good Programme

  • Type Advising
  • Client Comic Relief
  • Year 2015

To really accelerate the use of tech for social good, we need more well-targeted grant programmes that support charities and mission driven organisations to test and build digital innovations. Comic Relief have a reputation for innovation and we’re enjoying working with their team, and the funded charities, to add Comic’s expertise to the techforgood community

Ufi Vocational Technology Programme

  • Type Advising
  • Client Ufi Charitable Trust
  • Year 2015

Post-16 vocational learning is of huge social and economic importance, yet it is often overlooked in favour of formal and academic education. The Ufi Charitable Trust is in a position to address this and we’re working with them to find and support the most innovative uses of technology, and to create wide-spread positive change through vocational learning

British Council

  • Type Research
  • Client British Council
  • Year 2015

We worked with Latimer Media Group as part of their exciting Innovation Labs project to produce a guide on digital innovation processes for British Council staff. It's based on the extensive co-creation work with young people across 6 European countries

Tech for Good Meetup Group

  • Partner Bethnal Green Ventures
  • Find out more http://www.meetup.com/techforgood/

As a growing sector, it’s crucial that people find ways to come together, learn from one another and create opportunities - whether newcomers or those already dedicated to socialtech. In partnership with Bethnal Green Ventures we run the techforgood #meetups, bi-monthly events that inspire, connect and help techforgood entrepreneurs get and give support
